Required Information for Job Postings

To post a job, please include the following information in your signed document on company letterhead (PDF format):

  1. Job Title: Clearly specify the job role (e.g., Sales Manager, Graphic Designer).
  2. Job Nature: Define if the job is full-time, part-time, freelance, or temporary.
  3. Job Description: Provide a detailed description of the role, including responsibilities and day-to-day tasks.
  4. Age Limit: State any age requirements, if applicable.
  5. Salary: Include salary details if you wish to disclose them, or mention if it's negotiable.
  6. Period of Contract: Specify the contract length (e.g., permanent, 1-year contract).
  7. Facilities Provided: Mention any benefits (e.g., health insurance, accommodation, transportation).
  8. Experience Required: Clearly state the minimum experience needed, if any.
  9. Required Language(s): List languages required or preferred for the role.
  10. Location: Indicate if the job is on-site (include the location) or remote.
  11. Educational Requirements: Specify any educational qualifications required (e.g., Bachelor’s degree in Marketing).
  12. Working Hours: Provide details about the working hours or shifts.
  13. Application Deadline: State the deadline by which candidates must apply.
  14. Additional Requirements: Mention any specific skills, certifications, or other requirements for the position.

Note: These details help candidates understand the role fully and apply only if they meet the qualifications, making the hiring process more efficient for you.

How to Submit Your Job Posting

  1. Prepare a Document: Download, print, and sign our job posting agreement on your company letterhead. This must include all the required information listed above.
  2. Send It Over: Email the signed document to jobs@aj1st.com. Our team will review and verify your submission.
  3. Verification and Posting: Once verified, we’ll publish your job listing on our vacancies page, where it will reach thousands of job seekers.

Important: Our portal only accepts formal, verified postings to maintain a high-quality, secure platform for employers and job seekers.
